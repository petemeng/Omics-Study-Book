# 推荐阅读

## 入门框架

- Conesa et al. 2016. A survey of best practices for RNA-seq data analysis. *Genome Biology*.
- Stark, Grzelak and Hadfield. 2019. RNA sequencing: the teenage years. *Nature Reviews Genetics*.
- Luecken and Theis. 2019. Current best practices in single-cell RNA-seq analysis. *Molecular Systems Biology*.

## 单细胞与空间

- Macosko et al. 2015. Highly parallel genome-wide expression profiling of individual cells using nanoliter droplets. *Cell*.
- Zheng et al. 2017. Massively parallel digital transcriptional profiling of single cells. *Nature Communications*.
- Stuart et al. 2019. Comprehensive integration of single-cell data. *Cell*.
- Ståhl et al. 2016. Visualization and analysis of gene expression in tissue sections by spatial transcriptomics. *Science*.
- Asp et al. 2020. Spatially resolved transcriptomes: new tools for tissue exploration. *BioEssays*.

## ATAC 与表观基因组

- Buenrostro et al. 2013. Transposition of native chromatin for fast and sensitive epigenomic profiling. *Nature Methods*.
- Buenrostro et al. 2015. Single-cell chromatin accessibility reveals principles of regulatory variation. *Nature*.
- Bock et al. 2016. Quantitative comparison of genome-wide DNA methylation mapping technologies. *Nature Biotechnology*.

## 免疫组库与微生物组

- Robins et al. 2009. Comprehensive assessment of T-cell receptor beta-chain diversity. *Blood*.
- Georgiou et al. 2014. The promise and challenge of high-throughput sequencing of the antibody repertoire. *Nature Biotechnology*.
- Caporaso et al. 2010. QIIME allows analysis of high-throughput community sequencing data. *Nature Methods*.
- Callahan et al. 2016. DADA2: high-resolution sample inference from Illumina amplicon data. *Nature Methods*.
- Quince et al. 2017. Shotgun metagenomics, from sampling to analysis. *Nature Biotechnology*.

## GWAS 与多组学整合

- Visscher et al. 2017. 10 years of GWAS discovery: biology, function, and translation. *American Journal of Human Genetics*.
- Manolio et al. 2009. Finding the missing heritability of complex diseases. *Nature*.
- GTEx Consortium. 2020. The GTEx Consortium atlas of genetic regulatory effects across human tissues. *Science*.
- Cano-Gamez and Trynka. 2020. From GWAS to function: using functional genomics to identify the mechanisms underlying complex diseases. *Frontiers in Genetics*.

## 蛋白质组、代谢组与整合

- Aebersold and Mann. 2016. Mass-spectrometric exploration of proteome structure and function. *Nature*.
- Gillet et al. 2012. Targeted data extraction of the MS/MS spectra generated by data-independent acquisition. *Molecular & Cellular Proteomics*.
- Sumner et al. 2007. Proposed minimum reporting standards for chemical analysis. *Metabolomics*.
- Hasin, Seldin and Lusis. 2017. Multi-omics approaches to disease. *Genome Biology*.
